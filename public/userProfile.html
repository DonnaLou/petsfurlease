Experience with animals:

I am a retired Accountant enjoying sunny San Diego. I would love the opportunity to spend time with your furry friend. While I do not currently have my own, I have had the privilege of sharing my life with dogs in the past. They were my babies and I'll treat yours as if they are my own. I enjoy filling my day by exploring the local parks and scenery - your 4-legged friend is welcome to tag along if they are an explorer as well. If they are more of a stay-at-home type (a couch-canine) we can do that too. And if your baby has special needs (injections or oral meds) please let me know - I have experience administering both. I live in a secure apartment building downtown which has a 40 pound weight limit for all animals. I have a cat named Max that shares my apartment. He is a Maine Coon who is a typical grumpy cat, but harmless.
